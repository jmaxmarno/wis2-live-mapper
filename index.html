About WIS2 Live Mapper

A live, browser-only viewer of the WMO Information System 2.0 (WIS2) notification stream. The app connects to one of the WMO global brokers over MQTT-over-WebSocket and plots incoming messages on the map as they arrive.

Three message categories:

  • Origin — data published by the source centre
  • Cache — data mirrored by a global cache node
  • Monitor — broker heartbeats and processing alerts

Click any marker — or a topic's ⋯ icon — to open the full message payload (raw JSON) along with deep links into the WIS2 Global Discovery Catalogues (Canada, Germany, China). The ⚠ badge on a topic surfaces buffered messages that arrived without geometry (typically monitor alerts).

Markers fade to grey after the configured fade duration but stay on the map until the buffer's FIFO cap evicts them. The side-panel topic tree reflects what's currently buffered, so counts decrement as messages age out.

References: WIS2 Cookbook · Notification Message Schema
